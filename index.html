BeatSwipe is a free web platform for music producers to share swipeable beat portfolios via bio links. Fans can discover beats, save favorites, and purchase directly from producers' external stores.

2. Overview

We process personal data only as needed to operate BeatSwipe — account login, producer profiles, saved favorites, beat uploads, and optional newsletter emails. We do not sell your data and do not use third-party advertising or analytics trackers.

Producers see aggregate counts of portfolio page views, saves, and buy-link clicks for their own page only. These stats do not identify individual visitors and are not shared with ad networks.

3. Data we collect

  • Account: email address, password (stored hashed by our auth provider — we never see your plain password)
  • Profile (producers): producer name, bio, avatar image, social links (Instagram, BeatStars, SoundCloud, YouTube)
  • Favorites: beats you save to your account
  • Beat uploads: metadata (title, genre, BPM, buy links) and MP3 preview files you submit
  • Newsletter: email address, if you opt in separately
  • Technical: IP address and server logs from our hosting provider (for security and operation)
  • Local storage: login session, swipe progress, cookie preference, invite flag (stored in your browser)
  • Portfolio stats (producers only): anonymous event counts (page views, saves, buy clicks) — no fan names or emails
  • Session storage: one page-view count per portfolio per browser session (deduplication only)

4. Purposes & legal bases (Art. 6 GDPR)

  • Account & favorites: performance of our service (Art. 6(1)(b))
  • Producer profile & uploads: performance of our service (Art. 6(1)(b))
  • Portfolio stats: legitimate interest in giving producers basic feedback on their page (Art. 6(1)(f)) — aggregate only, no visitor profiles
  • Newsletter: your consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — withdraw anytime
  • Sign-up: your consent to store your email (Art. 6(1)(a))
  • Security & hosting logs: legitimate interest in operating a secure service (Art. 6(1)(f))

5. Processors (sub-processors)

We use trusted providers to run BeatSwipe. Each processes data only on our instructions:

  • Supabase — authentication, beat catalogue, database, file storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel — website hosting (Privacy Policy)
  • Make.com — newsletter signup automation (Privacy Policy)
  • jsDelivr — open-source script delivery for the optional QR code feature (Privacy Policy)

6. Third-country transfers

Some processors may store data outside the EU/EEA (e.g. USA). Where this applies, transfers are safeguarded by EU Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms offered by the provider.

7. Cookies & local storage

We use no tracking or advertising cookies. Essential storage includes:

  • Supabase login session (localStorage) — required for signed-in features
  • Swipe progress & favorites (localStorage) — improves your experience
  • Cookie banner preference (localStorage)
  • Portfolio view deduplication flag (sessionStorage, one entry per portfolio per session)

You can clear local data in your browser or via Profile → Clear local data. Signing out removes your login session. To delete your account and all associated data, use Profile → Settings → Delete account.

8. Embedded content & external links

Beat previews may load from YouTube or SoundCloud when you play or swipe a card. Those providers may receive technical data (e.g. IP address, device info) under their own policies. Buy links (BeatStars, Splice, etc.) take you to third-party sites we do not control.

9. Retention

  • Account data: until you delete your account
  • Newsletter: until you unsubscribe
  • Server logs: typically up to 30 days (hosting provider default)
  • Pending beat submissions: until approved, rejected, or deleted
  • Portfolio stats events: retained while the producer account exists; deleted when the account is deleted

10. Your rights

Under GDPR and Austrian DSG you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Delete your data (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time (without affecting prior processing)
  • Lodge a complaint with the Austrian Data Protection Authority (dsb.gv.at)

Contact us at hellobeatswipe@gmail.com for any request. We respond within 30 days.

11. Children

BeatSwipe is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

12. Changes
